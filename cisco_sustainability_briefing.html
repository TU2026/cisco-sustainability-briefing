SEA Energy
Per Tracxn data published April 20, 2026, Southeast Asia's energy transition sector attracted $1.8B in cumulative funding, but solar ($1.1B, 62%) and EVs ($505M) dominated. 🏪 Cisco Supply Chain Relevance
The underinvestment in battery storage and grid infrastructure in Vietnam, Indonesia, and the Philippines creates a compounding risk: suppliers in these countries may struggle to access reliable renewable electricity for their operations even if they commit to RE targets. Cisco's supplier RE enablement programs should account for grid reliability constraints and consider direct investment or offtake guarantees as part of supplier development.
